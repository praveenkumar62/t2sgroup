T2S Learning

T2S Learning, the founding division of T2S Group focuses in exponentially improving the employability potential of students, both in K-12 and higher education.

T2S Learning offers various knowledge and skill enhancement programs with thorough focus on emerging technologies and life skills.

T2S Learning enlists only current industry experts for any learning intervention.

T2S Learning has touched the lives of more than 100,000 learners.

T2S Learning, with its extensive expertise in large scale training interventions, is the natural and inevitable choice for both corporate and academia.

T2S Learning Offers

  • T2SAT, which is a technology training and assessment tool.
  • T2SAT has more than 25000 questions in its dynamic repository
  • Technology Ecosystem for Higher Education & K-12.
  • Ecosystem contains conceptual training, assessments, & placements services.
  • Ecosystem Mapped to academic topics, with inputs from industry leaders.
  • K-12 Ecosystem contains additional modules for school children from Industry.
  • K-12 Ecosystem contains practice modules for important competitive exams.
  • Intensive face to face training program by practicing industry professionals.
  • Proven Soft skills & Aptitude training by veterans.
